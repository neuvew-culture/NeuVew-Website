Artificial Intelligence

We are recruiting secondary school students for our introductory course on artificial intelligence.

First Lesson: Thursday, July 16, 7:30pm-9:00pm
Location: one-north Launchpad

Step into the AI age with a practical introduction to modern artificial intelligence, its applications, and the people, tools, and ethics shaping the future.

Syllabus

  1. The AI Age Has Arrived

    AI in everyday life, industry, academia, government, and military, etc.; what is AI?

  2. Chatting with AI

    Basic applications: querying information and learning knowledge; prompting basics.

  3. Classical AI vs Machine Learning

    Two different approaches to AI: a historical perspective.

  4. How Machines Learn

    Data, neural networks, and training.

  5. Ethics and Risks

    Fairness, copyright, privacy, and harmful content.

  6. Python for AI

    Basic data structures, operations, control flow, and functions.

  7. Large Language Models and Generative AI

    Basic principles and characteristics.

  8. Beyond Chatting

    Advanced applications: writing, translating, coding, analysis, and problem solving.

  9. Building Your Own Mini-Agent

    AI workflows and autonomous task execution.

  10. Multimodal AI: Images and Speech

    AI that sees, paints, and speaks.

  11. AI in Education and Job Transformation

    New ways of learning and working.

  12. The Future of AI

    Economic revolution and philosophical debate.
