Book Publication

Books are a complementary way to share knowledge, experiences and insights in addition to short courses. Compared with short courses, books offer a more in-depth exploration of topics and can be referenced at your own pace.

In this first phase, we focus on publishing our own books. In the future, we plan to collaborate with other authors to expand our offerings. Our own books are already covering a wide range of topics, from science and technology through art and literature, to philosophy and history.

We have obtained professional publishing expertise to ensure the quality of our books, but at the same time, we want to experiment novel design approaches. For us, style is as important as substance.

Technology in an Alternative Modernity

Ready to order

Technopoly and the monopoly of Western culture are the two distinct phenomena that define the modern age. This book challenges both.

Therefore, philosphy of technology and modernity theory constitute the core of this work. Through critial analysis of Western modernity, the book sketches a basic framework of an alternative modernity. In this framework, all the existing theories of technology are examined. With a sythesized theory of technology, technopoly is deconstructed.
