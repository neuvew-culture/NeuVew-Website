Short Courses

Our short courses usually contain a set of 10-15 sessions. Each session lasts one to two hours. The sessions are normally held on weekends or evenings to accommodate busy schedules, on weekly basis.

As we believe in the value of physical interaction, we try to teach in person whenever possible. But sometimes online sessions are also offered for convenience and flexibility. In either way, we strive to build strong connections between teachers and students.

When sharing knowledge, we emphasize deep understanding. More important than knowledge sharing, we focus on hands-on practice and creative ways to apply knowledge to real-world scenarios. And beside knowledge and skills, we also cultivate critical thinking and the ability to understand and cooperate with others.

Photography

Ever since the camera was integrated into mobile phones, photography has become an all-time and ubiquitous activity. Everybody wants to take better photos. Our photography courses are also designed for everybody.

To suit different needs and empower personal growth, we divide our photography courses into three levels: introductory, intermediate and advanced. At the beginning the students just need a mobile phone. As they progress, they can use more advanced equipment and will show more creativity in their work.

To emphasize hands-on practice and real-world application, our photography courses are held in the field, usually parks, rather than in a classroom. Each session is like a real-world field trip.

Artificial Intelligence

With the fast advancement of artificial intelligence (AI) in recent years, human history has rapidly stepped into the AI age. Our AI courses are meant to help ordinary people to prepare for this new age.

In the AI age, AI technology is just the foundation. On top of it, many aspects of society are being transformed. Accordingly, our AI courses are not just about technology, but also about understanding the broader implications of AI on our lives and society.

Our introductory course helps the students to build a comprehensive understanding of the AI age. Besides in-depth understanding of AI concepts and mastering of AI tools, the course also emphasizes the ethical and social dimensions of AI. Further courses will build upon this foundation.

Why Choose These Courses?

An enriched life is not a rich life, although material wealth constitutes a necessary condition for a good life. An enriched life is rich in a special sense, abundance of experiences, fine tastes, insights and creative ideas, and broad vision. What matters more is the spiritual fulfillment.

An enriched life is a full life, in the sense that all aspects of it are well-developed. We have in mind four dimensions of life: strength, warmth, breadth, and depth. Different area of culture contributes to each dimension. Science and technology grow our strength, arts and literature enhance our warmth, geography and history expand our breadth, and finally philosophy and religion increase our depth.

Modern society puts too much emphasis on material wealth and hence science and technology are often prioritized over other areas of cultural development. As a result, modern life ends up very skewed. Our courses aim to correct this imbalance and help construct a more natural life.

Therefore, all the cultural dimensions are granted equal importance in our curriculum. Each one is designed in view of balancing these dimensions. Taken together, they provide a comprehensive and balanced approach to personal development.
