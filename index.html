Cultivating an Enriched Life Through Learning, Travel, and Publishing

Creative and critical short courses, immersive and enjoyable learning trips, and inspirational and thought-provoking books for insatiably curious minds.

Our mission is to foster a new view in this fast changing and restless age, to obtain confidence in the time of uncertainties.

And finally, a new view will bring a new world！

This is our vision: A diverse and colorful world，with us playing an important role in it.

About Us

We are a group of insatiably curious learners, makers and explorers. We love reading, writing, making gadgets, creating art works and adventuring in the wide world.

We are eager to share our knowledge, experience and skills in short courses. We enjoy making new friends and exploring the world together on learning trips. We believe in the irreplacable value of books even in this digital age. They bring depth, peace and confidence to our lives. Therefore, we are also passionate about writing, designing, and publishing books.

So join our learning journey today! Let's explore and create together.

Short Courses

Our short courses are designed to enrich people's lives, not aimed at any particular exams, competitions or certificates, although they could be essentially helpful for concrete life goals.

In this way, they are not restricted to school students, but suitable for adults as well. We foster a life learning environment and try to stimulate curiosity no matter the age. Even retirees can benefit from our courses.

The scope of our courses is wide, covering various areas, including technology, arts, history, philosophy and religion. While facilitating fundamental inquiries, we keep pace with the latest developments.

Learning Trips

We believe that diverse experience and wide vision are essential for an enriched life. Travelling to different places allows us to gain new perspectives and broaden our understanding of the world.

Each of our learning trips is multi-faceted, containing both natural beauty and cultural diversity. While enjoying the scenery, the participants also marvel at the technological achievements, appreciate the art creations and relive the historical moments.

Our groups are always intimate and amiable. We exchange all kinds of ideas on the trips. Shortly after the trips start, strangers often become good friends.

Book Publication

In this digital age, book reading has become a rare and precious activity. Immersed in instant messages and various video feeds, people find little time for deep, reflective reading, let alone writing.

However, we believe that books are essential for organizing ideas and building deep understanding. Thoughts become clearer in writing a book and get systematically stimulated in reading a book. Hence, we think books are indispensable for increasing the depth of human life.

The books we publish also cover a wide range of areas, from science and technology to arts and literature, from life and history to philosophy and religion.
